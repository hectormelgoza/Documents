Tamper Evident Pouches
Tamper Evident bags are pre-sealed above the zipper and bottom fed, making unauthorized access to the protected object easily detectable. In many industries, such as food, pharmaceuticals and medicine, it is vital to know that the product has not been altered since it left the manufacturer. Our Tamper Evident bags are widely recognized as the most trusted and secure tamper evident bags on the market today.

These bags are also less labor intensive when loading compared to a regular zipper style bag. Loading the bag from the bottom and heat-sealing the bag can save time and money compared to loading a conventional zipper style bag through the top.

Please note that these MylarFoil bags are intermittently produced throughout the year according to customer demand, and as such may or may not be available.
