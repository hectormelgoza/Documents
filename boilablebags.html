Boilable / Microwavable Bags

Designed specifically for exposure to high temperatures, these 4.0 mil boilable / microwavable bags are FDA approved for food products and ideal for submersion in boiling water or being placed inside of a microwave. Suitable for vacuum sealing, these bags are often used to store tuna, dehydrated foods, pre-cooked commodities, and instant meals. Many of our customers use these bags for camping trips and other outings, advance meal preparation, and a variety of other applications. For businesses that deal in packaged food products, these bags are perfect for Meal-In-A-Pouch applications (think K Rations and MREs).

This is our only instock boilable / microwavable bag. For more information please contact us at: sales@sorbentsystems.com
