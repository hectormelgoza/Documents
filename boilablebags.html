    • Boilable / Microwavable Bags

    FDA Approved, High Temperature Pouches

    Intentionally engineered to withstand high temperatures, these 4.0 mil boilable/microwavable bags possess FDA approval for food products, making them an optimal choice for immersion in boiling water or utilization within microwave ovens. These bags are particularly suitable for vacuum sealing and are commonly employed for preserving tuna, dehydrated foods, pre-cooked commodities, and instant meals. A significant number of our clientele favor these bags for diverse purposes such as camping excursions, meal prepping in advance, and an array of other applications. For enterprises involved in the packaging of food products, these bags present an impeccable solution for Meal-In-A-Pouch scenarios, reminiscent of K Rations and MREs.

    In summary, these FDA-approved, high-temperature-resistant bags offer versatile and reliable packaging solutions for various food products, making them an excellent choice for businesses and individuals alike.
