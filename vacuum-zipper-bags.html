 IMPAK's ZipSeal™ technology ensures the freshness of your product up to five times longer than the average vacuum zipper bag. Our unique design maximizes bag suction, reducing machine stress and guaranteeing a positive seal.

These vacuum zipper bags can be used to package a wide range of items for storage and/or resale. Whether you are planning to package food products for sale or distribution, or you are using vacuum bags for the storage of sterilized medical supplies, IMPAK has a suitable vacuum zipper bag for your application. If your application requires the use of a vacuum bag size or style that is not listed here, IMPAK can easily create a custom design to meet your packaging needs. Simply contact our sales department at sales@sorbentsystems.com outlining the specifics of your particular application and someone will get back with you quickly. For standard vacuum bags, please check out or Vacuum Bags Page.

All of our vacuum bags are manufactured from durable FDA approved film and may be used for both food and non-food packaging.

Common Applications Include:
  • Meat and fish
  • Dehydrated fruit
  • Sterilized medical supplies
  • Electronics, such as transformers, resistors and circuit boards
  • Clothes storage
