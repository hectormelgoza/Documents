Hands free sealers come in three versions; single sided heat HF sealer, double sided heat HF sealer, and always hot HF sealer. Each model has features and characteristics that make that unit appropriate for specific application. The table top impulse sealers (stapler style sealers) are inexpensive and you can’t beat them for cost, but they are not efficient. A hands free sealer is beneficial in saving time, money, and having better product quality.

  



*Height adjustment available. Height modification adds 10" to the standard height of our impulse sealers


    


How to determine which type, model, and size is appropriate for your need? There are only three kinds of these in the world.

  • Double (two sided) Impulse Sealer
  • Single (one sided) Impulse Sealer
  • Constant (always hot) Heat Sealer
With your purchase of a HF sealer it comes complete with a pedestal, stand, adjustable work table, and foot control. What kind of seal do I want my machine to make?

Constant Heat Sealers - always hot HF sealer

Some industrial customers in the medical field and military packaging require the use of constant heat. Some customers like the constant heat sealers make a very wide strong hermetic seal. Other customers and product producers prefer the very clean yet still strong and resilient look of a 5mm or even 10mm wide impulse seal.
