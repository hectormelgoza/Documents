Molecular Sieve

Molecular sieve, also known as synthetic sodium alumina silicate is a unique adsorbent with a very unique pore structure. This product can be developed so that differentiation in actual pore openings is as small as one angstrom (1 angstrom = 1/1000 of a human hair). It is by far the best choice in terms of desiccant for companies looking to maintain the lowest possible relative humidity.

Molecular sieves retain their ability to adsorb water molecules over a much wider spectrum of temperatures than other desiccant materials. Molecular sieves also have a much higher equilibrium capacity for water vapor under very low humidity conditions. Molecular sieves contain a uniform network of crystalline pores and empty adsorption cavities, which give it an internal adsorptive surface area of 700 to 800 gm2 (1/2 the total volume of the crystals). Because of its uniform structure, molecular sieves will not desorb moisture into the package as readily as silica gel or clay as temperatures rise.

Unlike indicating silica gel, which turns from blue to pink as it becomes saturated with moisture, indicating molecular sieve turns from blue to beige (see picture above).

Uses for Different Sized & Pored Silica Gel

1-3 mm narrow pore silica gel - IMPAK P/N 13NP639SG05:

  • Used for drying items and in moisture proof packaging as a desiccant
  • May also be used as a catalyst carrier in solution
  • It is known to have high adsorbing and dehydration abilities and used to separate gasses depending on the pore size of the gel

1-6 mm wide pore silica gel - IMPAK P/N 16WP639SG03

  • This size is also known as "cat sands"
  • Frequently used as a cat litter alternative, as it is highly effective at reducing odors
  • Has many industrial uses:

    1. Purification and dehydration of gases produced on an industrial level
    2. Adsorption of complex proteins in the industrial fermentation process
    3. Removal of organic acids and high polymers in transformer or insulation oils
    4. Also seen as a basic monomer for complex silica gel products

2-5 mm narrow pore - IMPAK P/N 25NP639SG05

  • Used for drying items and in moisture proof packaging as a desiccant
  • Used as a catalyst carrier for reactions
  • Frequently used as a cat litter alternative, as it is highly effective at reducing odors
  • A precursor to silica powder. Best gel to ground down for powder use

Silica Gel Flour - IMPAK P/N 400SGF03

  • Silica flour has a wide range of uses in the commercial world:
    1. Paints, ceramics, soaps, toothpaste, skin care products, pharmaceuticals
    2. Used in cabels
    3. Often used in building materials and products
  • When added to plasters for molding it increases the range of functioning temperatures from 1300°F to 1450°F
  • Used as filler in epoxy resin castings and adhesives
  • Also possesses abrasion resistant properties
