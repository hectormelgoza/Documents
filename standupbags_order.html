    Stand up pouches are the ideal solution for food and pet food packaging, offering a good alternative to cans or aluminum trays. Stand up bags are produced from light-weight laminates and possess high oxygen and moisture barrier properties.

    Our stand up bags are available in a wide variety of formats (e.g. single serve packaging) and for a number of applications (e.g. food pouch, pet food pouch, etc.). Retort pouches can be sterilized and pasteurized for food, pet food as well as non-food applications and are customizable in shape, design and special features.

    The flexible concept of our printed stand up pouches also gives you a wide range of presentation possibilities to choose from. High-quality flexo print designs ensure an elegant, attractive appearance and catch the attention of potential buyers

    When determining what kind of stand pouch you will want, the two primary criteria that any buyer should consider are:

    Style

    Two primary style choices - stand up pouches with ZipSeal™ and stand up pouches without ZipSeal™. There are other style details such as valves, special bottom seals, and fitments which include the insertion of pour spouts into the stand up pouch.

    Material Choice

    We have over 53 standard and special flexible films on the floor at anytime. Available colors: silver foil, gold foil, black foil, blue foil, white foil, all clear and VISTA™
