Stand-up pouches (SUP) are the ideal solution for food and pet food packaging, offering a good alternative to cans or aluminum trays. Stand up pouches are produced from light-weight laminates and possess high oxygen and moisture barrier properties.

Our stand up pouches are available in a wide variety of formats (e.g. single serve packaging) and for a number of applications (e.g. food pouch, pet food pouch, etc.). Retort pouches can be sterilized and pasteurized for food, pet food as well as non-food applications and are customizable in shape, design and special features.

The flexible concept of our printed stand-up pouches also gives you a wide range of presentation possibilities to choose from. High-quality flexo print designs ensure an elegant, attractive appearance and catch the attention of potential buyers

When determining what kind of stand pouch you will want, the two primary criteria that any buyer should consider are:

Style

Two primary style choices - stand pouches with ZipSeal™ and stand pouches without ZipSeal™. There are other style details such as valves, special bottom seals, and fitments which include the insertion of pour spouts into the stand pouch.

Material Choice

We have over 62 standard and special flexible films on the floor at anytime. Available colors: silver foil, gold foil, black foil, blue foil, white foil, all clear and VISTA™

Light-weight and functional packaging

Compared to traditional cans pouches have less packaging weight per 100g of filled product and less shipping weight (3% of the filled product, as compared with 20% for metal cans). The drastic weight reduction of a stand-up pouch benefits both end-consumers and our environment with less waste, less carbon and less costs.

Thanks to the reduced retort time for a stand up pouch, the vitamins, minerals and the original taste of the meal are also preserved more fully. High-performance laminates ensure excellent barrier properties of the flexible pouch and keep content safe from oxygen, moisture and light.
