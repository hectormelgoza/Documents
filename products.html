Flexible Packaging: Mylar® Bags, Stand Up Pouches, Vacuum Bags & Much More

Flexible packaging has become increasingly popular with brand owners and consumers for its many advantages over rigid packaging. These include space savings, shipping savings, source reduction, materials savings, all while providing excellent product protection via high barrier materials that block light, moisture, oxygen and contaminates. This format appeals to consumers looking for on-the-go convenience, and brands appreciate the reduction in materials for both economic and environmental reasons.

IMPAK serves industries from pharmaceutical brands who need sterile, high barrier packaging, to food and beverage brands looking to stand out on shelves and deliver convenience. Flexible packaging is especially popular with subscription service brands who have a green initiative and benefit from lighter weight packaging that saves in shipping costs.

Popular flexible packaging options include stand up pouches, Mylar® foil pouches, Mylar® zipper bags, spout bags, liquid/gel packets, vacuum bags, boiling bags, fin seal bags, foil lined kraft bags, roll stock tubing and much more. Many of our customers start with a stock order and move to custom printed packaging.

MYLAR® is manufactured by DuPont Teijin Films.

Sorbents: Desiccants, Oxygen Absorbers, Silica Gel Paper, Indicators and more...

A sorbent is a material designed to remove undesirable substances, such as moisture, oxygen or gas, either through adsorption or absorption. Common examples include oxygen absorbers, or oxygen scavengers, which absorb oxygen molecules most typically by way of iron oxidation; and desiccants, which adsorb moisture and/or gas through binding with and trapping the molecules.

IMPAK Corporation is the clearly recognized leader in specialty sorbent production and engineered applications. Our molded desiccant product: CM900, can be engineered and produced to fit into specific applications in the demanding aerospace and medical field. Other specialty sorbents include our high heat desiccants in capsule form and heat resistant pouches.

Indicators that can reflect moisture content from 5% to 100% as well as an oxygen indicating tablet that will recognize and indicate the presence of oxygen at 0.1% or less are also hallmarks of the technology developed within the company.
