Box Liners

Box liners are an integral part of packaging for many industries, including commercial fisheries, poultry processing, primary AND secondary packaging for pharmaceutical products, and the shipment of hygroscopic materials such as machinery, powders and other moisture sensitive items.

For a variety of reasons, packages are often delayed in shipment, left out in the elements, exposed to harmful bacteria and hazardous chemicals, and damaged. Unfortunately, it is not always possible to control the conditions your package is exposed to, but with our Mylar box liners, you can minimize the damage caused by these adverse conditions.

To provide the best protection for your merchandise, it is important to take the following points into consideration:

  • Oxidation – Is your product prone to oxidize when continuously exposed to oxygen?
  • Condensation – Are you working with a hygroscopic power or water sensitive electronic device?
  • Dust – With biomedical and sterile equipment, it is imperative that dust and small particles be kept out of contact with the product.

Box liners, with the addition of our internal atmosphere creating sachets, can provide any enclosed environmental conditions you require. Our in-stock box liners are made from durable Mylar foil material, range in size from 5” x 5” x 12” to 17” x 17” x 12”, and in thickness from 2.5 mils to 5.0 mils. If your needs fall beyond these dimensions and/or thickness, give us a call; IMPAK has the ability to design and produce a box liner to suit whatever dimensional needs you may have.
