    • Box Liners

    Also Known as Gaylord Liners

    Box liners play a crucial role in the packaging of various industries, encompassing commercial fisheries, poultry processing, primary and secondary packaging for pharmaceutical products, as well as the transportation of hygroscopic materials like machinery, powders, and other moisture-sensitive items.

    Due to numerous factors, packages frequently experience delays in shipment, endure exposure to harsh weather conditions, encounter harmful bacteria and hazardous chemicals, and suffer from damages. Regrettably, it's not always feasible to control these external influences on your package. However, by utilizing our Mylar box liners, you can significantly mitigate the harm caused by these adverse conditions.

    Our box liners, combined with our internal atmosphere creating sachets, offer the flexibility to create any desired enclosed environmental conditions. These ready-to-use box liners are constructed from robust Mylar foil material, available in various sizes, ranging from 5” x 5” x 12” to 17” x 17” x 12”, and thickness options spanning from 2.5 mils to 5.0 mils. Should your requirements exceed these dimensions or thicknesses, don't hesitate to Contact Us. We possess the capability to design and produce custom box liners tailored to meet your specific dimensional needs.

    Ensuring optimal protection for your product requires careful consideration of the following points:

    • Oxidation – Is your product prone to oxidize when continuously exposed to oxygen?
    • Condensation – Are you working with a hygroscopic power or water-sensitive electronic device?
    • Dust – With biomedical and sterile equipment, it is imperative that dust and small particles be kept out of contact with the product.
