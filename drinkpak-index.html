Flexible packaging provides a cost-saving alternative to traditional rigid containers for beverages. Many manufacturers are switching to flexible materials for the sake of source reduction, increased shipping efficiency, space saving, and edge-to-edge printing capability. Whether it's single-serving beverages or gallon packs to serve big groups, flexible pouches are increasingly appearing on retail shelves and at restaurants.
