SpoutPak™ Liquid Spout Bags​

The SpoutPak™ is the latest innovation in the continuing evolution and development of stand up pouches and packaging technology with the integration of closures into the flexible package itself. IMPAK's SpoutPaks feature a recloseable spout which is hermetically sealed to the packaging material. This spout protects the product through its lifecycle and they can be used for both liquids and powders. We have the capability to support investment in proprietary closures and the tooling to produce them.
