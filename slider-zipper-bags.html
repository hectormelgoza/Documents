    • Slider Zipper Bags

    Stay organized with slider zipper bags. These multi-purpose clear zipper bags will help you compartmentalize any endeavor you undertake. Wether you want to organize your legos or simply want to clean up your refridgerator, Impak's slider zipper bags will help you get the job done. Our slider zippers are also available with clear stand up bags which serve well for baking goods. Whatever your need may be, these easy to open and close slider bags are perfect for storing food, knickknacks and more. These bags are also durable and reusable which makes them a direct competitor to Ziploc and other clear zipper bag companies.
