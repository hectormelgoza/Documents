    Home/Lab Vacuum Sealer

    Introducing the KeepFresh Home Commercial Vacuum Sealer KF108, formerly known as VS-280! This innovative vacuum sealer combines the advantages of compact design and exceptional efficiency. Despite its size, this rugged unit boasts features typically found in expensive industrial vacuum sealers. With a retractable metal nozzle, dual side by side seal, and a reliable solid-state seal timer, it delivers top-notch performance.

    The KeepFresh KF108 is a versatile tool that finds its application in various domains, from quality control laboratories in electronics and food processing facilities to the homes of homemakers, fishermen, backpackers, and collectors of books, coins, and more. The best part is, it is the ONLY low-cost vacuum sealer that doesn't require the use of special bags, making it both economical and user-friendly. Experience the convenience and effectiveness of the KeepFresh Home Commercial Vacuum Sealer KF108 today!

    With this vacuum sealer, you can seal bags up to 10.8" wide.
