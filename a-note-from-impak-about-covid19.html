A Note from IMPAK about COVID-19

As many businesses and supply chains have been severely affected by the continuing COVID-19 pandemic, we want to assure you that IMPAK will remain open and operational. Due to the crucial role we play in the medical and food supply chain, IMPAK is considered an essential business by all state and local governments.

The health and safety of our employees, families, customers, and vendors are our highest priority and measures are in place to ensure this.

Our team continues to practice social distancing and good hygiene practices while processing our clients' orders. We are working hard to ensure our clients continue to have the necessary support needed to get through this.

Due to current health ordinances, there will be NO returns on any packaging products or related items. If you are unsure of a product you may need, please call your sales rep and they will help you.

We will continue to monitor the constantly changing situation.

Thank you. We are all in this together!
