Heavy Duty Mylar® Bags

Also Known as IronShield™ Bags (7.0 mil and up)

The term IronShield™ is an innovation of IMPAK to refer to Mylar® bags possessing better than average moisture / oxygen barrier properties and puncture resistance. At a minimum thickness of 7.0 mils, these heavy duty Mylar® bags are nearly twice the thickness of a standard 4.3 mil Mylar® bag. Field tested and proven under adverse and varied conditions, these heavy duty Mylar® bags are suitable for virtually any industrial application.

Looking for sterile pouches? Learn more

The following list of heavy duty Mylar® bags is by no means indicative of IMPAK’s complete production capabilities; many of our customers have unique packaging requirements. Whether you need an in stock industrial grade Mylar® bag, or your needs go beyond the norm, IMPAK can provide a solution. With specialized die cutting equipment, rollstock, and custom print and labeling capabilities, IMPAK can meet virtually any packaging need. For more information, please contact our team.

MYLAR® is manufactured by DuPont Teijin Films.
