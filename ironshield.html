    • Heavy Duty Mylar® Bags

    Also Known as IronShield™ Bags (7.0 mill and up)

    Introducing IronShield™, a term coined for Heavy Duty Mylar® bags that exhibit exceptional moisture and oxygen barrier properties, as well as outstanding puncture resistance. These heavy-duty Mylar® bags boast a remarkable minimum thickness of 7.0 mils, nearly doubling the standard 4.3 mil Mylar® bags. Rigorously field-tested and proven effective under adverse and varied conditions, these robust Mylar® bags are an optimal choice for virtually any industrial application.

    Please note that the list of heavy-duty Mylar® bags provided below does not encompass all of our production capabilities. We understand that many of our customers have specific and unique packaging requirements. Whether you require an industrial-grade Mylar® bag from our available stock or if your needs demand custom solutions, we are fully equipped to provide the perfect answer. Our specialized die cutting equipment, rollstock options, and custom print and labeling capabilities enable us to meet virtually any packaging need. For further details, please don't hesitate to reach out to our dedicated team. orders@sorbentsystems.com
