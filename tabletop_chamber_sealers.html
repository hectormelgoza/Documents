    • Tabletop Chamber Vacuum Sealers

    The Gramatech line of CHTC table-top model vacuum chamber sealers represent the smallest machines in our vacuum chamber sealer line. However, do not let their size fool you. These machine very versatile and ready to tackle a wide variety of vacuum and vacuum / gas flush applications. As their name implies these machines are meant to be used on any open table space or countertop thus saving valuable floor space in your packaging area. All Gramatech table-top models feature all 304 stainless steel construction including a heavy gauge fully welded vacuum cavity that prevents warping that can occur on the lighter gauge deep drawn vacuum cavities used in many other brands. All standard Gramatech CHTC models operate on standard 110V/1PH/60HZ power and do not require an additional compressed air source.

    The Gramatech table-top vacuum chamber sealers are simply the best units on the market and the entire range of models is certified and labeled that they meet UL, CSA and NSF standards. All models utilize the standard of the industry Busch industrial quality vacuum pumps and feature our PD-01 basic digital control system that is not only very operator friendly but also very accurate and if your application could benefit from other features like memory, soft air venting or multi-stage gas flush all Gramatech table-top models can be upgraded to our PNC-01 microprocessor control system.

    Therefore, if you need to package anything from cannabis to computer chips or anything in between on a smaller scale with truly professional results Gramatech has a table-top vacuum chamber sealer to meet your needs.

    For further information concerning the best Gramatech model to handle your application give us a call today and speak with an IMPAK equipment specialist.

    Benefits of a Tabletop Chamber Sealer

    labelTabletop models offer you a self-contained (110V/1PH/60HZ only no outside source of compressed air required) machine with a small footprint yet capable of providing a completed package with the lowest residual oxygen content.

    label• Very little operator input is required to produce a perfect package every time. Just place your package inside the vacuum cavity and close the lid to start the cycle.

    labelIf your package or packages fit inside the vacuum cavity and on the seal bar of seal bars then your product can be vacuum packaged in one cycle no multiple nozzles required, except in gas flush applications.

    labelAll tabletop models can be upgraded to our PNC-01 microprocessor control system thus adding the capability to have memory capacity as well as multi-stage gas flush and soft air venting.
