    • Clear Vacuum Seal Bags

    Vacuum grade materials and pouches are a notable strength of IMPAK Corporation. From the standard 3.0 mil structure (some companies go as low as 2.8 mil and call it 3.0) to 4.0 and 5.0 mil laminations designed for hot fill or automatic pouch filling systems, vacuum grade pouches* can be used to package a wide range of items. Whether you are planning to package food products for sale or distribution, or you are using vacuum bags for sterilized medical supplies, IMPAK has a suitable pouch for your application. If you require a vacuum bag size or style that is not listed here, IMPAK can easily create a custom design to meet your packaging needs. Simply contact our sales department at sales@sorbentsystems.com with the specifics of your particular application and one of our representatives will respond to you.

    Common Applications Include:

    - Jerky
    - Meat and fish
    - Nuts and edible seeds
    - Dehydrated and freeze dried food
    - Sterilized medical supplies
    - Electronics, such as transformers, resistors, and circuit boards
    - Plastic resins and filaments
    - Clothes storage

    *What is a vacuum grade pouch?

    It's a pouch that can hold a vacuum, whether it's with a simple, entry-level machine like our Hippo Vacuum Sealer, that is found everywhere from medical laboratories to sushi bars, up to our WorkHorse Vacuum Sealer with iS2 (Intelligent Sealing System) vacuum control.

    WorkHorse Vacuum Sealer

    Looking for Zipper Vacuum Bags or Textured Vacuum Bags?

    All of our vacuum bags are manufactured from durable FDA approved food grade film and may be used for both food and non-food packaging.

    Not Just Under Vacuum

    Due to the high barrier properties required for a quality vacuum seal pouch, they also make good all-around packaging for your a wide variety of needs. Our Moisture Sensitive Product Storage System was designed to provide a very low Water Vapor Transmission Rate for electronics and other moisture sensitive goods using 8.5" x 13" Superpoly™ vacuum seal pouches as the base.
