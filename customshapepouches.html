    • Uniquely Shaped Pouches

    We are a leading innovator in the production of uniquely shaped bags. Brands are turning to unique shapes for their packaging as a way to attract consumers, promote brand identity, and in many cases, to improve the functionality of the package.
    - Liquid and gel squeeze packets have a dispenser tip for better precision when handling
    - Cone shaped bags are now the standard for popcorn and candy
    - Round mini bags offer a memorable package format for single-serving applications
    The above are just a few examples of the thousands of unique shaped bags IMPAK has produced for customers with specific packaging needs. See our list of ready-to-order unique bags below.

The high barrier material is ideal for packaging sensitive materials because it offers protection from moisture, light, gases and other contaminates. The high quality film is also resistant to tears and punctures.
