    • Uniquely Shaped Pouches

    We are a leading innovator in the production of uniquely shaped bags. Brands are turning to unique shapes for their packaging as a way to attract consumers, promote brand identity, and in many cases, to accomplish a function of the package better. Liquid and gel squeeze packets, for example, offer a funneled tip for better precision compared to a square pouch; Cone shaped bags are now standard for popcorn and candy; And round mini bags offer a memorable package format for single-serving applications. The above are just a few examples of the thousands of unique shaped bags IMPAK has produced for customers with specific packaging needs. See our list of ready-to-order unique bags below.

    Applications for Uniquely Shaped Pouches IMPAK offers all the unique sizes and shapes you see below, and has the ability to produce custom packages. *Custom print, size, color and material available by order.
