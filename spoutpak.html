    • SpoutPAK™ - Spouted Pouch

    Liquid Pouch Bags

    The SpoutPAK™ represents a groundbreaking advancement in the ever-evolving realm of stand-up pouches and packaging technology, seamlessly incorporating closure mechanisms into the flexible packaging itself. These versatile pouches find widespread application in packaging liquids, including beverages for takeout, cold brew coffee, alcoholic drinks, automotive fluids, cleaning solutions, lotions, and more. Their appeal extends to beverage lovers, serving as ideal drink pouches and multi-serving containers for beverages like coffee, tea, lemonade, and adult drinks such as wine, spirits, and cocktails.
    Our SpoutPAK™ range showcases pouches equipped with reclosable spouts, hermetically sealed to the packaging material to ensure product preservation throughout its life cycle. These SpoutPAK™ Stand Up Pouches are versatile enough to accommodate both liquid and powder contents. In addition to the core SpoutPAK™ line, we offer specialized variants like the BarrelPAK™, DrinkPAK (large liquid pouches with handles), and the CubePAK™ cheertainer, which lends itself to bag-in-box applications. Furthermore, we possess the expertise and resources to facilitate the development of proprietary closures and the associated tooling for production.

    The highlighted items have squared corners and are offered at a discounted price. Due to overwhelming customer demand, future production of those items will have rounded corners.

    Custom Pouches: Many pouches are produced to customer specifications with a choice of sizes, colors, printed and unprinted. Get started with our Custom Print Form
