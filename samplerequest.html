Sample Requests

IMPAK offers samples to help customers with sizing. If you have material questions or are looking for custom packaging, contact our product specialists for guidance.

There are four (4) items which MUST be completed:
1.) WHAT ARE YOU PACKAGING? (YOUR PRODUCT)
2.) QUANTITY: We will provide 1 sample of almost any pouch that measures 10" x 16" or smaller. If your request includes a pouch larger than 10" x 16" you will be contacted by a representative who will provide pricing information.

NOTE:


We can provide larger quantities for a nominal fee. Please fill out the "Notes/Comments" section below with any special requests.


3.) FED EX ACCOUNT NUMBER (REQUIRED)     What if I don't have an account number?
