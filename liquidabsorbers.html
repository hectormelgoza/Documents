Special Surface Bags		 Super-absorbent Liquasafe™ liquid absorbers easily absorb up to 300 times their own weight in aqueous solutions for the efficient containment of spills. In the presence of fluid, the specially formulated Liquasafe™ packet quickly bursts open, releasing a super-absorbent polymer powder which quickly absorbs and immobilizes a wide range of solutions. The resulting gelatinous material is easily contained by an outer vessel, shipping container or some other such device.

Benefits of using Liquid Absorbers are:
  • They are easy to use and fast-acting
  • They immobilize a wide range of solutions
  • They are available in a variety of sizes to fit specific needs and applications
  • They are an effective solution for emergencies



Typical Applications:

  • Aqueous solutions
  • Dilute alkalis
  • Biohazardous fluids
  • Toxic solutions
  • Urine specimens
  • Etiologic agents
  • Radioactive solutions
