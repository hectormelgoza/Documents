    • Liquid Absorbers

    Super-absorbent Liquasafe™ liquid absorbers easily absorb up to 300 times their own weight in aqueous solutions for the efficient containment of spills. In the presence of fluid, the specially formulated Liquasafe™ packet quickly bursts open, releasing a super-absorbent polymer powder which quickly absorbs and immobilizes a wide range of solutions. The resulting gelatinous material is easily contained by an outer vessel, shipping container or some other such device.

    Liquasafe is the perfect liquid absorber for hospital and janitorial use, or any spills that need to be quickly handled, such as at grocery stores.
