    • Hot Jaw™ - Constant Heat Sealer

    We have two types of Hot Jaw™ Constant Heat Sealers. One is a PTFE-Covered, synthetic fluoropolymer, unit which produces a narrow seal but will work with polyethylene. The other is a PTFE-Coated, synthetic fluoropolymer, unit with its non-stick surface making it an excellent unit for large foil Mylar® bags or covers.

    Most common heat sealers deliver a seal of approximately 2mm which is less than 1/10th of an inch. We sell these units but we also sell Hot Jaw™ units that have a seal width of about 14.2mm which is about 9/16th of an inch.

    The jaws are capable of closing any type of heat sealable multi-layered* structure. You can also make your own sized bags with this sealer!
