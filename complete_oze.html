Open Zipper End (OZE) Pouches
All of our OZE bags can be heat sealed above the ZipSeal to provide an ultra-airtight enclosure. When packaged properly (i.e. the product heat sealed inside of an OZE pouch with an appropriately sized oxygen absorber), these bags are virtually impervious to factors which contribute to microbial growth and spoilage.

Please note that these MylarFoil bags are intermittently produced throughout the year according to customer demand, and as such may or may not be available.
