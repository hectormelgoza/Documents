    • Open Zipper End Pouches

    ** Open Zipper End pouches are top fed. Unless otherwise stated, ALL pouches are Outside Dimension **

    Open Zipper End (OZE) ZipSeal™ Bags are closed on 3 sides and open at the zipper end. This means they may be filled and closed without requiring a heat seal. They are an alternative to the ZTE, or Zipper Tamper Evident bags, which are filled from the bottom and should be sealed thereafter.
