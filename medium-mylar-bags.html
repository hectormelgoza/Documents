    • Medium Mylar® Bags

    3-Side Seal Bags & Fold-Over Bottom Bags: 6" - 15"

    Our Medium Mylar® Bags encompass barrier bags that are larger than 6" and smaller than 15" in size. Feel free to consult our experts regarding personalized films and various dimensions for your specific requirements.
    orders@sorbentsystems.com

    All of our Mylar® bags are crafted from food-grade material that is FDA approved, with the exception of static shielding bags. These bags possess exceptional barrier properties, making them widely utilized in food-related industries. However, they are also highly favored in non-food sectors, including chemicals, pharmaceutical compounds, medical devices, supplements, adhesives, automotive parts, machinery, electronics, telecommunications, defense, aerospace, exports, and various other applications.

    Custom Options - Whether you need printed or unprinted Medium Mylar® Bags, we can supply pouches to your specific dimensions. We also offer a wide range of print capabilities including matte/gloss finishes, frosted films, and much more. Custom Print Form

    Standard 3-side seal pouch, with or without load lip
    The three-side seal pouch is by far the most popular form of packaging due to its simplicity and virtually limitless number of applications. These bags come in many sizes and colors and can be used for the safe packaging of practically any type of product.
    *IMPAK also produces laboratory-grade bags of this type with write-on capability, as well as custom print pouches.

    Fold-over-bottom pouch (2-side seal)
    Fold over pouches offer slightly more filling volume in the same outer-dimension bag. The three closed sides are achieved by a folded side, and two sealed sides.
